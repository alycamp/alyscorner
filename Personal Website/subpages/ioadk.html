Infections of a Different Kind (Step 1)

AURORA, 2018

Introduction

Where do I even start. This wasn't my first introduction to AURORA, I had previously heard a few songs off of her first album All My Demons Greeting Me as a Friend and thought, "Hey. This is pretty cool.". That was around winter of 2018 while I was studying for my grade 10 business exam. It wasn't until summer of the next year that I listened to this album. And oh boy, was it just the beginning of something amazing. This entire album is beautiful in ways that I can't even describe, just shy of perfect (in comparison to it's sister album that was released the following year). It's so bright, yet so bleak at times. And overall a beautiful collection of feeling (almost primal).
